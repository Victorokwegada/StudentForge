How it works

A simple flow students can understand quickly.

1. Build

Students complete projects in school, bootcamps, hackathons, or personal learning.

2. Upload

They fill a clean form with title, links, screenshots, tools used, and category.

3. Get seen

Communities, peers, and recruiters can explore their work in one place.
